# What is Riichi Mahjong?

Riichi is the standard Japanese variant of mahjong — a four-player game of
drawing and discarding tiles to assemble a winning hand of four sets and a pair.

## The shape of a hand

A standard winning hand is **four sets + one pair**:

- A *set* is either three identical tiles (a triplet) or three in sequence of
  the same suit (a run).
- The *pair* is two identical tiles.

## Why "riichi"?

When a player is one tile away from winning with a closed hand, they may declare
**riichi**, betting 1,000 points for a shot at bonus value. It's the namesake
mechanic of the ruleset and the reason scoring rewards closed, disciplined play.

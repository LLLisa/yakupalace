# Tile artwork

The SVG tiles in this directory are from **tile-art** by WarL0ckNet:
https://github.com/WarL0ckNet/tile-art

They are based on raster images from martinpersson.org, vectorized and
refined. Licensed under **GPL-3.0**.

Filename convention (used by `src/features/memorization/components/Tiles.tsx`):

- Suited: `1man`…`9man`, `1pin`…`9pin`, `1sou`…`9sou`
- Red fives: `akaman`, `akapin`, `akasou`
- Winds: `tan` (East), `nan` (South), `xia` (West), `pei` (North)
- Dragons: `haku` (white), `hatsu` (green), `chun` (red)
- `back` — face-down tile

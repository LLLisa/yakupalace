# About Me

My name is Lisa and I live in New York. I am a software engineer
by trade and a musician, cyclist, and general nerd otherwise. I learned how
to play riichi from Dave, the owner of [Sparrow's Nest](https://sparrowsneststudio.com/)
in Manhattan. Then I stopped playing for a decade while the NYC
riichi scene was taking off. I started playing again in early 2026,
only to find that the amount of skill and strategy out there now was quite overwhelming.

So I started this app/blog/site to start sorting through the theory and strategy that
I'm learning on this journey, in hopes that it will help others as well.

You can find me at most tournaments and Tryhard Tuesdays at Sparrow's Nest,
occasionally at [Riichi Nomi](https://www.riichinomi.com/) meetups,
on Mahjong Soul as TinaFromTV, and a not-zero amount of time
on [Twitch](https://www.twitch.tv/tinafromtv).
